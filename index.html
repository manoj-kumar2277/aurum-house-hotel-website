Our Philosophy

Fewer rooms. Closer attention.

We keep Aurum House small on purpose. With only forty-two suites, every guest is known by name, every request is remembered, and every detail — from the weight of the linen to the pour of the evening aperitif — is considered on purpose, not by default.

Our team trained across Europe's finest maisons before bringing that discipline home, pairing it with a distinctly local warmth you won't find in a chain.
